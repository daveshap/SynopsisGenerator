A group of super-powered individuals in Germany find themselves at odds with each other in the far future. As they attempt to sort out their differences, they are pursued by a powerful and evil force that wants to use their powers for its own nefarious purposes. The heroes must overcome their differences and learn to work together in order to defeat the enemy and save the world from destruction. Along the way, they discover that they have more in common than they thought, and they develop a deep bond of friendship. The heroes triumph over the evil force, but at a great cost. They are left battered and weary, but they have saved the world and can now finally rest.
