England, early Renaissance. A young boy, jungle opera singer, is orphaned and goes to live with his grandparents in the jungle. He grows up learning about the jungle and its ways, and developing a love for the animals and plants. When he is a teenager, he leaves home to go on a journey to find his father, who he believes is still alive. He travels through the jungle, overcoming many challenges along the way. He meets many different people, including a tribe of cannibals who take him in and eventually eat his father. He also meets a group of hunters who teach him how to survive in the jungle. After many years, he finally finds his father, who is living in a treehouse in the jungle. They are reunited and live happily ever after. The boy and his father live happily ever after in the jungle.
