The story begins in Egypt during the Bronze age. A young woman, who is full of energy and vigor, is living in a small village. One day, she hears gossip about a man who is having an affair with a married woman. The young woman is intrigued and decides to follow the man to see what he will do next. The man leads the young woman to an empty house in the village. It is clear that he is planning to meet his lover there. The young woman hides and watches as the man and woman arrive and begin their tryst. However, things quickly take a turn for the worse when the husband of the woman arrives and catches them in the act. The husband is enraged and begins to beat the man. The young woman intervenes, trying to stop the violence. However, the husband turns on her, and she is forced to flee. The young woman is left to wonder what will happen to the man and his lover. Will they be able to continue their affair, or will the husband's jealousy tear them apart?
