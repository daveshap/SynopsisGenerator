When a young woman is found brutally murdered in a small Indian village, the local constable is assigned to the case. However, he quickly realizes that he is in over his head and turns to his friend, a retired detective, for help. Together, they begin to piece together the woman's final hours, and uncover a web of secrets that leads them to the killer. As the investigation continues, the detectives uncover more and more evidence that points to a wealthy, powerful man as the killer. However, they have no concrete proof and know that taking him on will be a dangerous undertaking. They must decide whether to risk everything to bring him to justice, or let him get away with murder. In the end, the detectives are able to amass enough evidence to build a case against the man and he is brought to trial. However, during the trial it is revealed that the real killer is someone else entirely. The detectives had been so focused on taking down the man they suspected that they had overlooked the true murderer. Nevertheless, justice is served and the killer is brought to justice.
