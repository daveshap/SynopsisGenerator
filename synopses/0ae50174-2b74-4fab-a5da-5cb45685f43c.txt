Xena, a fierce and skilled warrior, is living in a time of legendary conflicts. She has allied herself with the great king Hercules, and together they are fighting to defend their homeland from invaders. One day, they receive a distress call from another planet, which is under attack by vicious creatures. They rush to the aid of the planet, and find themselves outnumbered and outmatched. However, they are determined to save the planet, and using their expert skills, they triumph against the creatures. In the process, they forge a strong friendship with the planet's inhabitants. As the planet comes under threat from another invasion, Xena and Hercules rally the people to defend it once again. This time, the odds are even more stacked against them, but they refuse to give up. In the heat of battle, Xena is severely injured, but she refuses to retreat. Instead, she rallies the people to continue fighting, and together they emerge victorious. After the successful defense of the planet, Xena and Hercules bid farewell to their new friends and return to their own homeland. They have formed a strong bond, and they know that they will always be there for each other, no matter what challenges they face.
