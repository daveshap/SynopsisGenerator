It is the year 1250 BC in England, and the land is in turmoil. The Bronze Age is coming to an end, and the people are desperate to find a way to survive. They turn to a new leader, a strange and eccentric man who seems to have the answers to their problems. The new leader, known as the Quirky One, quickly becomes a powerful figure in England. He rallies the people to his cause, and together they begin to build a new society. However, not everyone is happy with the Quirky One's ideas, and soon there is unrest within the ranks. As the people of England struggle to adapt to the Quirky One's unconventional ways, they must also deal with the looming threat of invasion from a neighboring country. The Quirky One's leadership is put to the ultimate test as the people of England fight for their survival.
