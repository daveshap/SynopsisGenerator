The story begins in space, aboard a large spacecraft. It is the year 4th century BCE and the Asia is the setting. The spacecraft is piloted by an unlikely character, an unlikeable and somewhat gruff man who is not very sympathetic. He is on a mission to find a new planet for humans to live on, as Earth is no longer habitable. The man's journey takes him to many different places and he has many adventures. He meets both friendly and unfriendly aliens, travels to different galaxies, and witnessing firsthand the decline of civilisation on Earth. He also meets a woman, a beautiful and enigmatic woman who he falls in love with. Eventually, the man reaches a new planet which is perfect for humans to live on. He begins to set up a new civilisation there and starts to build a new life with the woman he loves. As the new civilisation starts to take shape, the man and his wife have a child. Their child is a prodigy and is able to communicate with other intelligent beings in the universe. Through his child, the man is able to learn about the true nature of the universe and the reasons behind why Earth became uninhabitable. The man also discovers that there are other civilisations in the universe who are also looking for new planets to inhabit. He realises that if humans are to survive, they need to find a way to stop these other civilisations from taking over their new planet. To defend their planet, the man and his wife train their child to become a great warrior. The child grows up to be a powerful fighter and eventually leads the humans to victory against the other civilisations. With the help of his child, the man is able to create a new and better civilisation on the new planet. The planet is now a thriving and prosperous place, and the man and his wife live there happily for the rest of their days.
