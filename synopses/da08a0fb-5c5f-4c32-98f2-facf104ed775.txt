On another planet, in a land steeped in magic, two young women meet and fall in love. Though their families and friends are not supportive of their relationship, they are determined to make it work. However, when one of them is taken away by an evil queen, the other must go on a dangerous quest to save her. The young woman sets out on her quest, journeying to far-off lands and encountering many challenges along the way. She meets new friends and creatures, all of whom help her on her quest. Slowly but surely, she gets closer to the evil queen's castle. The young woman faces the evil queen in a final battle. She defeats the queen and rescues her love. They return home to a hero's welcome and finally get the chance to live their lives together openly and happily.
