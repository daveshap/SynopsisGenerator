A young woman named Aotearoa is living in a peaceful village in New Zealand in the distant future. The world is a very different place than it is today, and Aotearoa is content with her simple life. However, she often feels like she is missing something. One day, Aotearoa meets a mysterious stranger who tells her that she is destined for greatness. He also tells her that there is a great danger coming to her village and that she must leave immediately. Aotearoa is hesitant at first, but eventually decides to go on an adventure with the stranger. Meanwhile, in another time period, a group of young people are also preparing for a journey. They are all mischievous children who often cause trouble, and they are excited to embark on their quest. The leader of the group is a boy named Arthur, and he is determined to find a lost treasure. As Arthur and his friends search for the treasure, they repeatedly run into trouble. They also meet a girl named Aotearoa, who joins their quest. Together, they have many thrilling adventures and finally find the lost treasure. Throughout their journey, Aotearoa and Arthur slowly fall in love with each other. However, they are from different time periods and know that they can never be together. They say goodbye to each other, knowing that they will never see each other again. Aotearoa returns to her village and lives out her days in peace. Arthur also returns to his time period and eventually becomes the king of Britain. Although they are living in different times, they often think of each other and the adventure they shared.
