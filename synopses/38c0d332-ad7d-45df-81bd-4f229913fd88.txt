When the nine-tailed fox spirit, Tsukiko, is born in the mountains of Asia, she is regarded as a bad omen by her village. Fearing her powers, they banish her from the village and she is taken in by a family of farmers who live on the outskirts of the village. While living with her new family, Tsukiko discovers her powers of shape-shifting and begins to use them to help the family with their farm work. However, she is constantly shunned by the villagers who view her as a monster. One day, when Tsukiko is out in the forest collecting firewood, she spots a group of bandits attacking a group of travelers. Using her powers, she summons a giant wolf to scare off the bandits and save the travelers. Grateful for her help, the travelers invite her to join them on their journey to the Emperor's palace. On the journey, Tsukiko befriends a young man named Kenshin who is also on his way to the palace. Kenshin is fascinated by her powers and the two quickly become close friends. When they reach the palace, they are both presented to the Emperor who is impressed by Tsukiko's abilities. The Emperor asks her to stay and help him protect the kingdom from the demons that have been attacking it. Tsukiko agrees to help the Emperor and starts using her powers to defend the kingdom. She quickly gains a reputation as a powerful creature who can vanquish any demon. demons begin to fear her and avoid attacking the kingdom. One day, a particularly powerful demon named Orochi comes to the palace and demands that the Emperor surrender the kingdom to him. When the Emperor refuses, Orochi attacks the palace. Tsukiko tries to stop him but is no match for his power. Just when it seems like Orochi will defeat her, Kenshin arrives and helps her to drive him off. After Orochi's defeat, the Emperor makes Kenshin and Tsukiko both nobles and they marry. As the years go by, they have children and grandchildren and Tsukiko continues to use her powers to defend the kingdom. The kingdom flourishes under their protection and becomes a peaceful and prosperous place.
