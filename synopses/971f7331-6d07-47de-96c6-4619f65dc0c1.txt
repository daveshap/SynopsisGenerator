In the small English town of Shepherdstown, life is anything but idyllic. poverty and crime are rife, and the locals are all too familiar with the brutal reality of life. But for one young girl, Lily, things are about to change. Lily is a shy and withdrawn child, but she has a special gift. She can see into the future, and she is able to use her gift to help those around her. When she foresees a local boy, Jack, being killed by a gang of thugs, she is able to warn him in time and save his life. This act of kindness sets off a chain of events that will change Lily's life forever. As she uses her gift to help others, she begins to build up a following, and people start to see her as a kind of messianic figure. But not everyone is happy with Lily's newfound status. The gang of thugs she thwarted are out for revenge, and they will stop at nothing to punish her. Meanwhile, the police are also becoming interested in Lily, and they are determined to find out what she knows about a series of unsolved crimes. As the pressure mounts on Lily, she finds herself in danger of losing everything she holds dear. but she refuses to give up, and with the help of her friends, she sets out to uncover the truth and bring justice to her town. ENDING: Lily's efforts are finally successful, and she is able to expose the true perpetrators of the crimes she solved. The gang of thugs are brought to justice, and Lily is hailed as a hero by the people of Shepherdstown. With her gift, Lily is able to help others and make a difference in her community. She has finally found her place in the world, and she is able to live happily and peacefully knowing that she has made a difference.
