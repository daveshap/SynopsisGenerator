In the near future, New Zealand is the setting for a biker media landscape. A group of brooding, morose characters contemplate the world around them, often haunted by past traumas or mistakes. Through their interactions and individual journeys, they each begin to realize the severity of the social issues at hand. As the story progresses, the characters' individual plights intensify, resulting in a griping conclusion. The tone remains sobering throughout, with occasional moments of levity provided by the sarcastic wit of some of the characters. In the end, the characters are left changed by their experiences, providing the reader with a new perspective on the issues addressed in the story. The style remains gritty, with violence and dark details providing an unsettling glimpse into the world of the biker media landscape.
