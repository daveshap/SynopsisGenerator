The story begins in the stone age, in a Mediterranean setting. The main character is a young girl from a racial minority group. She is living in a time when her people are being oppressed by a ruling class. One day, she decides to take action and fight back against the ruling class. She rallies her people and leads them in a revolt. The ruling class is overthrown and the young girl and her people are free. The young girl and her people celebrate their victory and begin to rebuild their lives. However, the ruling class is not going to give up easily. They launch a counterattack, and the young girl and her people are forced to fight for their freedom once again. The young girl is courageous and determined, and she leads her people to victory. The young girl and her people are finally free and they live happily ever after. The end.
