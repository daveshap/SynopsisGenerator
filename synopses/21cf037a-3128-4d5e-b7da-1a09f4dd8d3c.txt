Captain Antonio della Luna is the head of the Italian Space Police Force. A tough and experienced law enforcement officer, he is tasked with keeping the peace in a future where humanity has colonized the solar system. However, when a strange murder occurs on one of the space stations under his jurisdiction, della Luna is baffled. The victim, a popular holovision star, was killed in a very specific and brutal way. As della Luna and his team investigate the case, they quickly realize that this is no ordinary murder. The killer is clearly intelligent and methodical, and seems to be toying with them. With the pressure mounting, della Luna has to catch the killer before anyone else dies. But as the clues begin to pile up, he starts to realize that this case is connected to a much bigger conspiracy… As della Luna and his team race to solve the case, they are constantly derailed by new clues and dead ends. Every time they think they are getting close to the killer, they are met with another roadblock. The murders continue to mystify and horrify the people of the solar system, and the pressure on della Luna to catch the killer grows. Meanwhile, the killer seems to be getting closer and closer to della Luna himself. As the captain gets closer to the truth, he realizes that the killer is someone with a personal grudge against him… Della Luna finally comes face to face with the killer, and the truth is even more shocking than he could have imagined. The killer is someone from his own past, someone he thought he had left behind long ago. As the two men face off, della Luna must come to terms with the choices he has made in his life. And he must find a way to stop the killer before anyone else dies…
