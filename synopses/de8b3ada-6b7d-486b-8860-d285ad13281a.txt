In a distant future England, the world is a very different place. Scientists have managed to harness the power of the sun, and this has resulted in great advancements in technology. The sun's energy is used to power everything, from cars to phones to houses. However, there is one downside to this new world - the sun is so powerful that it is slowly killing the earth, and scientists have been unable to find a way to stop it. The story follows a group of friends - quirky and eccentric characters who are all struggling to come to terms with the impending doom of the earth. They all deal with it in different ways, but ultimately, they all have to come to terms with the fact that the world is going to end. This character-driven story is all about the interior growth and development of the characters as they deal with the difficult situation they find themselves in. It is a slowly intensifying story that culminates in a gripping conclusion.
