In the 18th century, the North Atlantic is a dangerous place. Sailors are constantly attacked by pirates, and many ships are lost at sea. To make matters worse, the waves are filled with deadly creatures that can kill a man in seconds. But for two friends, the danger is just another adventure. When their ship is attacked by pirates, the duo is separated. One is taken captive by the pirates, while the other is left behind to die. But the friends refuse to give up on each other, and they both set out on a dangerous journey to find each other and escape the North Atlantic. Along the way, they face deadly creatures, treacherous terrain, and ruthless pirates. But their bond is stronger than anything they encounter, and they ultimately find their way back to each other. The friends are finally reunited, and they set sail for home. But the North Atlantic is a changed place, and they know they'll have to face the dangers again one day.
