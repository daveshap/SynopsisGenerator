In a small village on the outskirts of a large kingdom, a young girl named Jena is born. Jena is born with a deformity that makes her body look twisted and her face ugly. Jena's parents are poor and can't afford to have her deformity corrected, so they do their best to hide her away from the world. Jena grows up knowing that she is different and that she will never be accepted by society. One day, when Jena is out looking for berries in the forest, she comes across a wounded bird. Jena nursed the bird back to health and decides to keep it as her pet. She names the bird Seren and Seren becomes Jena's only friend. One day, Jena's village is attacked by a group of bandits. Jena's parents are killed and her house is burned down. Jena manages to escape with Seren, but she is now alone in the world. Jena decides to go to the kingdom and find a way to make a living. On the way, she meets a man named Simon who agrees to help her. Simon is a skilled thief and he teaches Jena everything he knows. Together, Simon and Jena commit a series of daring heists in the kingdom. As their notoriety grows, they become known as the "Robin Hood" thieves. The people of the kingdom love them and they are admired by many. One day, Simon and Jena are caught in the act of stealing by the kingdom's guards. Simon is killed and Jena is captured. Jena is brought before the king and she is sentenced to death. Jena is about to be executed when she is saved by Seren, who attacks the guards and gives Jena a chance to escape. Jena flees the kingdom and vows to one day return and take revenge on the king. Jena spends the next few years living in the forest, learning how to fight and hone her skills. She also begins to develop a plan to take down the king. One day, Jena's plan comes to fruition and she leads a group of rebels in an assault on the kingdom. Jena faces the king in combat and she defeats him. Jena takes over the kingdom and becomes the new queen. Jena is finally able to put her deformity to rest and she can finally start living her life the way she wants to. With the help of her friends and allies, Jena is able to create a new and better kingdom where everyone is accepted and loved.
