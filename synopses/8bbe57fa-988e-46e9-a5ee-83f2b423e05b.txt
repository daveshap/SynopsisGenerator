Best friends Lily and Rose are both gifted fairies who have been recruited by the magical council to help keep the peace between the Realms. They are assigned to the Moon, which is currently in the midst of a civil war. Their mission is to help the rebel forces overthrow the evil Queen and her tyrannical rule. The girls arrive on the Moon and are quickly thrust into the midst of the conflict. They use their magic to help the rebels win several key battles, but the Queen is a powerful witch herself and she will not give up easily. As the war drags on, Lily and Rose come to realize that they are falling for two of the rebel leaders, Jasper and Luke. They must find a way to win the war and save their own relationship before it is too late. Lily and Rose continue to fight alongside the rebels, but they are also struggling with their own growing feelings for Jasper and Luke. The war takes a toll on all of them, and they are constantly in danger of being captured or killed by the Queen's forces. One day, while they are out on a mission, Lily and Rose are ambushed by the Queen's soldiers. They are taken captive and brought before the Queen herself. The Queen is furious that the two girls have been helping the rebels and she orders them to be executed. However, Lily and Rose use their magic to escape and they return to the rebel camp. The war finally comes to an end after a long and brutal battle. The rebels are victorious and the Queen is overthrown. Lily and Rose are finally able to be together with Jasper and Luke. They all settle down in the peace and quiet of the Moon and live happily ever after.
