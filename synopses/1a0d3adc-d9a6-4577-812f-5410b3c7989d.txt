Kate is a strong female urban fantasy character who lives in Australia during the late medieval period. She is a take-charge woman who is always at the top of her game. One day, she is approached by a group of people who want her to help them with a problem. They explain that they are having difficulty understanding and dealing with a sardonic new king who has taken over the throne. Kate agrees to help them and sets out to find the king. Kate eventually locates the king and begins to establish a relationship with him. She soon realizes that he is a very complex person with a lot of depth. Over time, she comes to understand him better and the two of them begin to work together to solve the issues that the people are having. They eventually succeed in resolving the problems and everyone is happy. Kate and the king continue to work together and their relationship grows stronger. They eventually fall in love with each other and get married. They live happily ever after and rule the kingdom together.
