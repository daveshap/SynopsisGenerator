The planet is in a mythic period, full of adventure and danger. A group of young friends, full of sarcasm and wit, decide to embark on a grand quest. They must travel across the globe, visiting different countries and overcoming challenges in order to find a great treasure. Along the way, they learn about different cultures and gain a new appreciation for their own country. With every new danger they face, they also grow as individuals, learning to work together and use their individual strengths to overcome any obstacle. In the end, they triumphantly return home with the treasure, having learned invaluable lessons and formed lifelong friendships.
