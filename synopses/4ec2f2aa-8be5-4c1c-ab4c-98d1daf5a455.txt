The America of Early Medieval times is a vast and largely untamed land, waiting to be explored and conquered. Into this land come a group of adventurers, each with their own motives and goals. Among them is a young woman named Flanna, who is seeking to find her place in the world. The group soon finds themselves in the midst of danger and intrigue, as they discover that they are not the only ones with an interest in the America of this time period. There are others who would claim the land for themselves, and who will stop at nothing to achieve their objectives. The group must band together if they are to survive the many challenges that await them. Along the way, they will face fearsome monsters, devious traps, and treacherous villains. But if they can overcome all of these obstacles, they just might find a way to triumph in the end. WORDplay-filled: The group's adventures are filled with clever uses of language, as they make their way through the many challenges they face. Along the way, they encounter puns, palindromes, anagrams, and other clever uses of words. These wordplay-filled adventures are sure to keep readers engaged from beginning to end.
