In the not too distant future, the world is a very different place. The planet has been through a cataclysmic event that has left it mostly submerged under water. In the aftermath, a new civilization has arisen. One that is very different from our own. Under the sea, a new society has formed. It is a society that is very advanced and has access to technology that we can only dream of. The main character is a woman named Sarah. She is a scientist who is working on a way to reverse the effects of the cataclysm. She is also the daughter of the leader of this new underwater society. Sarah is a strong and courageous woman who is determined to find a way to save her people. Sarah's efforts are met with resistance from some members of her society who believe that there is no way to change their fate. They believe that they are meant to live under the sea and that it is their destiny. Sarah is undeterred and continues to work on a way to reverse the cataclysm. Meanwhile, the underwater society is met with another threat. A group of marauders, who call themselves "The piranhas", are attacking and plundering the underwater villages. They are a ruthless and brutal group who take what they want without regard for the consequences. Sarah and her team are able to find a way to reverse the cataclysm. But, when they try to implement their plan, they are met with resistance from the piranhas. The piranhas are determined to stop them and will do whatever it takes to keep the underwater society from changing. Sarah and her team are also met with resistance from Sarah's father. He is not convinced that their plan will work and does not want to risk the safety of his people. Sarah is able to convince him to let them try and she leads her team into battle against the piranhas. Sarah and her team are successful in defeating the piranhas and reversing the cataclysm. The underwater society is saved and they are able to return to the surface. Sarah is hailed as a hero and she is able to reunite with her father. They are both able to start anew and look to the future with hope.
