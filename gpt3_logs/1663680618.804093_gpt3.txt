BEGINNING:

==========

The story opens with the main character, Sir Lancelot, preparing for a tournament. He is eager to win and impress his love, Lady Guinevere. However, he is quickly defeated and humiliated in the first round. Lancelot is distraught and returns to his home, castle Camelot. While Lancelot is sulking in his room, Guinevere comes to visit him. She comforts him and they eventually end up kissing. However, they are interrupted by a page who brings news that the King is summoning Lancelot to court. Lancelot leaves and Guinevere is left alone in the room. It is revealed that the King is dying and he has summoned Lancelot to his bedside. The King tells Lancelot that he wishes for him to take care of Guinevere after he is gone and to marry her. Lancelot agrees and the King dies shortly afterwards. Lancelot and Guinevere are both grief-stricken, but they try to comfort each other. They eventually get married and life goes on. However, things are not happy for long. Several years into their marriage, Lancelot begins an affair with another woman, Queen Margareth. This affair causes a huge rift between Lancelot and Guinevere and their relationship is never the same again. The story ends with Lancelot and Guinevere living separate lives in their own castles. Although they are no longer together, they still love each other deeply.
