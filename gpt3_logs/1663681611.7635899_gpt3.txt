markers from an ancient culture are discovered on the moon, nobody can quite agree on what they mean. Is it a message? A warning? A call to arms? The situation quickly deteriorates when the cultists take one of the astronauts hostage and threaten to kill her if the team doesn't do as they're told. The team is forced to give in to the cultists demands and they start making plans to leave Earth.
