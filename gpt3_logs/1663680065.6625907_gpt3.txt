The Brazilian military soon becomes involved, trying to fighting off the creatures and close the portals. But they're up against overwhelming odds, and it soon becomes clear that they need help. That's where our heroes come in. A group of unlikely allies - a street-wise teenager, a brilliant scientist, a renegade soldier, and more - come together to try and save the city. Faced with overwhelming odds, they'll have to use all their ingenuity and courage to succeed. But if they can overcome the creatures and close the portals, they just might be able to save the city - and the world.
