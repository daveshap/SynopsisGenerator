In the year 2200, Korea is a thriving sea-faring nation and the home of Choi Ji-Won, a young woman with a passion for adventuring. Despite her parents' warnings, Ji-Won has always been determined to follow in the footsteps of her hero, the great explorer Captain Yoon. So when Captain Yoon's ship, the Lucky Star, arrives in her hometown of Incheon, Ji-Won is determined to find a way aboard and join the crew on their latest expedition. Ji-Won's opportunity comes when she saves the life of the ship's cook, who has been injured in a fall. She is taken on as the ship's new cook's assistant and finally gets her chance to explore the world. The Lucky Star sets sail for the frozen north, where they hope to find a legendary lost city. But when they reach their destination, they find something much more dangerous than they ever could have imagined. MIDDLE: As the crew begins to explore the lost city, they quickly realize that it is not abandoned. They are soon attacked by a group of people who have been living there in secret. Outnumbered and outgunned, the crew is forced to retreat. But Ji-Won is determined to find out what lies at the heart of the city and why the people there are so desperate to keep it hidden. The crew makes another attempt to reach the city, this time led by Ji-Won. But when they get there, they find that the lost city is not what it seems. It is a heavily fortified outpost, guarded by a powerful energy weapon. The people of the lost city are not just trying to keep it hidden, they are trying to keep it safe. END: Ji-Won and the crew of the Lucky Star are finally able to make contact with the people of the lost city. They learn that the city is the last bastion of humanity, a refuge from a war that has devastate the rest of the world. The people of the city are fighting a desperate battle to keep the weapon safe, and Ji-Won and the crew of the Lucky Star are their only hope. Together, they fight to keep the city safe, and finally begin to rebuild the world that was lost so long ago.
