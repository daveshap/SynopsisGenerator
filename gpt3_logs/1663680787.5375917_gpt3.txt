best friends Daisy and May are shocked to find themselves stranded on a remote planet in another galaxy. With no way to get home, the girls must find a way to survive in a hostile land full of strange creatures. Daisy Hopes to find a way home, but May is content to make the best of their new home. As the girls learn to adapt to their new life, they discover that the planet is not as hostile as they thought and that they may have a chance to find their way home after all. MIDDLE: As the girls settle into their new life, they find themselves constantly pursued by the native creatures of the planet. May discovers a hidden cave where she meets a friendly creature she calls Hodge. The two girls soon discover that their new friends are the key to finding a way home. END: With the help of Percy and Hodge, Daisy and May finally find a way back to Earth. They say goodbye to their new friends, vowing to never forget the adventure they had in the 15th century.
