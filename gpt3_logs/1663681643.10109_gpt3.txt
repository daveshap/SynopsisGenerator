Eight-year-old Charlie is playing in the forests outside of his village in New Zealand when he stumbles upon a strange creature caught in a trap. The creature, a faerie, begs Charlie to help her and in return she will grant him a wish. Charlie agrees and sets the creature free. The faerie fulfills her promise and Charlie wishes to be the most daring and brave adventurer in the world. Charlie grows up to be a skilled warrior and earns the respect of his village. However, he quickly becomes bored with the simple life of a farmer and longs for adventure. One day, Charlie's wish comes true when he is recruited by a group of mercenaries to go on a quest to rescue a princess from a dragon. Charlie and the mercenaries travel to the kingdom of the princess and battle their way through hordes of monsters. They finally reach the castle where the dragon is guarding the princess. Charlie bravely fights the dragon and rescues the princess. The king rewards Charlie and the mercenaries with a large sum of gold and the princess's hand in marriage. Charlie returns to his village a hero and marries the princess. They live happily ever after and have many adventures together.
