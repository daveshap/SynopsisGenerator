The villager's listen in rapt attention as the stranger tells them about the series of natural disasters that befell their village in the original timeline. First, a severe storm damaged the village's boats and destroyed the fish stock. Then, a fire swept through the village, destroying most of the buildings. The villagers were forced to move to a new location, but the new land was not as fertile and they struggled to survive. Eventually, they were forced to turn to prostitution and piracy to make ends meet. The villagers follow the historian's instructions and are able to avert the first disaster. This gives them the confidence to face the second disaster, and they are able to prevent that from happening as well. With each disaster that they avert, the villagers become more and more hopeful that they can change their timeline and create a better future for themselves.
