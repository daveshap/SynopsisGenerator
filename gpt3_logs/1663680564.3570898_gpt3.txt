BEGINNING:

==========

3rd century Australia is a land of harsh beauty and great danger. The Bushrangers, a group of renegade Magic users, have been terrorizing the countryside, using their powers to terrorize and plunder. The people are terrorized and the king is desperate. He sends his best knights, led by Sir Gareth, to put an end to the Bushrangers. The knights are ambushed by the Bushrangers and are captured. Sir Gareth is able to escape and makes his way to a small village. He finds the villagers being terrorized by the Bushrangers and uses his magic to fight them off. His actions gain the attention of the villagers who decide to help him. SIR GARETH: Sir Gareth is a courageous and determined knight who is determined to put an end to the Bushrangers. He is skilled in magic and is not afraid to use it to protect the people. He is a natural leader and the villagers are drawn to him. VILLAGERS: The villagers are poor and terrified of the Bushrangers. They are willing to help Sir Gareth because they believe he can protect them. They are not skilled in magic but are willing to fight and die for their freedom. BUSHRANGERS: The Bushrangers are a group of Magic users who have turned to crime. They are vicious and ruthless, using their powers to terrorize the people. They are led by a woman named Morgana who is equally skilled in magic and violence. MIDDLE: As the days pass, Sir Gareth and the villagers prepare for the inevitable attack by the Bushrangers. They build fortifications and stockpile weapons. Sir Gareth also begins teaching the villagers how to use magic. One night, the Bushrangers attack. The battle is fierce but the villagers are able to hold them off. Sir Gareth is able to capture Morgana and she is brought before the king. King: The king is a wise and just ruler who is desperate to end the reign of terror by the Bushrangers. He is pleased when Sir Gareth returns with Morgana and gives him a large reward. Morgana: Morgana is the leader of the Bushrangers. She is a skilled magic user and is equally skilled in violence. She is captured by Sir Gareth and is brought before the king. END: The king sentences Morgana to death and she is executed. Sir Gareth and the villagers are hailed as heroes. The village is rebuilt and the people live in peace. Sir Gareth remains in the village, using his magic to protect the people.
