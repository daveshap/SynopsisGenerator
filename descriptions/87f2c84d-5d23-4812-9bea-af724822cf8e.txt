Special powers, cults, mind control, intense, suspenseful, action-packed,heart-pounding Jaylen Brooks is a 16-year-old girl who is taken in by a man who claims to be able to help her develop her abilities and teach her how to control her emotions and use her mind to influence the world around her, but she eventually realizes that he only wants her for her powers and she must stop him before he can make her do a mass suicide.
