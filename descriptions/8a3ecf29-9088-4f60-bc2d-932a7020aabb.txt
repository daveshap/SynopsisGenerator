In a small village in Africa, a man named Juju arrives and starts to perform strange rituals. The villagers are initially scared of Juju but, over time, they begin to see that he is actually helping them. However, Juju is possessed by evil spirits and starts to lead the village into chaos. The village is destroyed and Juju is the only survivor. Juju, shaman, Africa, tribe, spirit, ancestors, evil, darkness, chaos, bloodshed, thriller, horror, suspense.
