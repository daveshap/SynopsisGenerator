dark, stormy, atmospheric, suspenseful, written in first person, difficult journey, encourages self-reflection This story is about a man named Giovanni who is struggling with depression and anxiety. He starts to see a life coach named Sofia who helps him to make positive changes in his life. However, Sofia is killed in a car accident and Giovanni sinks into a deep depression. He is eventually involved in a car accident himself and goes into a coma. In a dream, Sofia tells him to wake up and live his life to the fullest. This gives him the strength to fight for his life and he eventually wakes up from the coma. He starts to live his life in a way that honors Sofia's memory.
